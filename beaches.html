South india is the area encompassing the indian provinces of Tamilnadu, Andhra Pradesh/Telangana, Karnataka, Kerala, Orisaa and union Territories Andaman Nicobar, Lakshadweep, Puducherry, the most populous city are Bangalore, Chennai, Coimbatore, Hyederabad, Kochi/Cochin, Vijaywada and Visakhapatnam, south india is famous for its ancient temples, beaches and othe historical places, here your can see full of diversity of cultures, languages, traditions etc, every province have their own lifestyle, festivals, foods and different regional languages in every states of south india.

Southern India is famed for it's temples, it's architecture and it's rich cultural heritage. Southern India's best known tourism destinations like Chennai, Mahabalipuram, Mysore etc., as well as several of Southern India's lesser known but equally rewarding destinations like Pondhicherry, Madurai, Coorg, Kabini, Hassan and Hampi. South India is famous for one more thing, its perfect weather to enjoy perfect vacations. For those who still believe in the phrase "Nothing is Perfect," they have not experienced the South Indian tours yet. There is a lot that one can find to have fun in South India. Whether it is taking sun baths at warm beaches, or surfing in the clear seas, South India has loads of surprises for its every visitor and its flamboyant hotels and ornate surroundings, South India makes a perfect get to go destination in affordable costs.

1

Day- 01 (CHENNAI/ARRIVAL)

Arrival at chennai international airport, it's biggest city of south india and the capital of tamilnadu province of india, you will be greeted by our representative then transfer to hotel followed by dinner and overnight stay.

2

Day- 02 (CHENNAI-MAHABALIPURAM)

After breakfast proceed for sightseeing of st goerge fort, art and gallery museum, st mary church, mylapore temple, drive through marina beach then have lunch in local restaurant, after that drive (45km/01hr) to mahabalipuram, on arrival check in hotel, dinner, overnight stay.

3

Day- 03 (MAHABALIPURAM)

Breakfast in hotel, after that go for sightseeing tour of mahabalipuram and kanchipuram, it's popular for their rock hewn temples, ancient architecture and sculptures, the fantastic shore temple is well known and most photographed temple in south india, during sightseeing have lunch local south indian foods in local restaurant, overnight stay in hotel followed by dinner.

4

Day- 04 (MAHABALIPURAM-PONDICHERRY-THANJAVUR)

This morning after breakfast drive (225km/05hr) to thanjavur, enroute visit pondicherry, it was a french colony till 1954 and still have distincly french flavour with its mediterrain waterfront and cobbled street, here you can visit aurbindo ashram, auroville and matri mandir, have lunch in local restauran later continue drive to thanjavur, it's is chola dynasty town famous for brahadeeswara temple a world heritage site by unesco, on arrival at thanjavur chech in hotel, dinner and overnight stay.

5

Day- 05 (THANJAVUR-TRICHY)

Breakfast in hotel after that sightseeing tour of brahadeeswara temple as well as other chola dynasty temples, later drive (55km/01hr) to trichy, it's also famous town for ancient temple town of tamilnadu province, on arrival check in hotel followed by lunch, later vist to rock fort and island of srirangam, back to hotel, dinner and overnight stay.

6

Day- 06 (TRICHY-MADURAI)

After breakfast drive (145km/03hr) to madurai, the city known for its rich culture, heritage and historical background, the most famous place here is meenakshi temple, enroute have lunch, on arrival check in hotel, in evening you can have a glimple of ceremony in meenakshi temple, back to hotel, dinner and overnight stay.

7

Day- 07 (MADURAI)

Breakfast at hotel followed by sightseeing tour of the most prominent temple of this region meenakshi temple, teppa kulum and tirumalainaik palace, have lunch in lacal restaurant and stroll around local market, come back to hotel follwed by delicious south indian dishes which is popular around in the world, overnight stay in madurai.

8

Day- 08 (MADURAI-CHENNAI/DEPARTURE)

Early morning after breakfast drive (460km/08hr) to chennai intrnational airport for connect flight for onward journey or you can fly from domestic airport madurai to cochin for onward flight.

END OF JOURNEY/BON VOYAGE
