Adventurous Place in Himachal

Bir-Billing is a village settled in the Joginder Valley of Himachal Pradesh. The place portrays a typical and idyllic village setting. However, very close to the village you will find an entirely different picture.

Where?

Bir-Billing is a Paragliding destination in the heart of Himalaya’s near famous tourist destination Dharamshala in Himachal Pradesh. This was “First Paragliding World Cup of India.”

How to Reach?

Billing is situated north of Bir at the distance of 14 km and gateway to the Thamsar Pass trek. Trek lead to most tribal region “Bada Bhangal” of Himachal Pradesh. Bir is mainly a Buddhist town and houses a monastery and a Tibetan handicraft center.
